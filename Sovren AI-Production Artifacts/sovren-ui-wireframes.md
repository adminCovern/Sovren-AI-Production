## 1. INTERFACE PHILOSOPHY

The SOVREN AI interface creates a **consciousness-integrated holographic command bridge** featuring the synthetic android intelligence (SOVREN AI) as the central neural nexus, with human-appearing executives arranged in operational formation. The dark theme with blue neural effects reinforces SOVREN's identity as "Synthetic Execution Intelligence."

- **SOVREN AI**: Translucent android form with blue neural pathways
- **Shadow Board**: Photorealistic human executives
